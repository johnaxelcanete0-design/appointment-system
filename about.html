ABOUT US

BMG, which stands for "By Maria Glenda", was founded in December 2020 by Maria Glenda Macatangay Valencia—a passionate entrepreneur who has always believed in the power of self-care and enhancing one’s natural beauty. What started as a personal love for beauty and wellness became a mission to help others feel confident and beautiful in their own skin.

With her dedication and vision, BMG quickly rose to prominence and has since become one of the most trusted names in the beauty industry in Batangas. Her passion and commitment were recognized when BMG received the "Most Outstanding Eyelash Extension and Microblading Services" from the Asian Sterling Awards.

From its humble beginnings, BMG has now grown into a thriving beauty brand with 9 branches spread across different parts of Batangas, continuously offering top-notch services and transforming lives—one beauty session at a time.
